Our verification standard

Every sale on this page has been confirmed by at least two of the following: Wall Street Journal real estate reporting, Redfin verified closed sales data, public county property records, licensed brokerage press releases, or reporting by Robb Report, Architectural Digest, or Inman News.

We do not include off-market sales that cannot be independently verified, estimates or assessments, listing prices (only closed sales), or sales from non-disclosure states where final prices are not public record. Texas, for example, is a non-disclosure state — even widely-reported sale prices cannot be confirmed from public records.

This page is updated as new records are set and as we complete verification for additional states. Last reviewed June 2026. Know of a verifiable state record we've missed? Send us the source.
